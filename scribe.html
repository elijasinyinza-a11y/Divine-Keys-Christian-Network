The Key of Intercession — How Prayer Unlocks Spiritual Doors

Every great move of God in scripture was preceded by a man or woman who would not stop praying. Daniel prayed three times a day and the heavens were moved. Elijah prayed with intensity and the rain came. The early church prayed in one accord and the Spirit fell with power.

Intercession is not a religious exercise. It is the mechanism by which heaven's agenda is released on the earth. When you intercede, you are not informing God of what He does not know — you are aligning yourself with what He has already purposed, and in doing so, you become the instrument through which He acts.

The divine key of intercession operates on three principles: persistence, authority, and agreement. Each principle unlocks a different dimension of God's power in your situation...
