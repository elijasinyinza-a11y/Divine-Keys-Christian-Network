Questions
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your Profile page. You retain Pro access until the end of your billing period.
What does "unlimited sessions" mean?
Pro members can start as many Sophia and Scribe sessions as they need — no monthly cap. Free members are limited to 10 sessions per month across both agents.
Is the price in USD?
Yes, pricing is in USD. The board is reviewing local currency options — check back for updates.
Does Pro include Divine Keys Publishing submission?
Pro includes the full publishing checklist and DOCX export. Formal submission to Divine Keys Publishing is handled through a separate publishing agreement with the editorial team.
