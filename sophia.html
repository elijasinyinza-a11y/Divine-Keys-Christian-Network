Romans 8:1 — No condemnation. This is Paul's great declaration after the struggle of chapter 7. The person who is in Christ Jesus is no longer under the verdict of the law.

"There is therefore now no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus."
Romans 8:1 (ESV)

The phrase "in Christ Jesus" is key — it's a positional truth. Your standing before God is determined not by your performance but by your union with Christ. Moving to verses 2–4, Paul introduces the law of the Spirit of life — a higher principle that liberates us from the law of sin and death.
